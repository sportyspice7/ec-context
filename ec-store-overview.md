## Overview

The Explore Clairemont store is the commerce arm of [ExploreClairemont.com](https://exploreclairemont.com), a hyperlocal community media site serving the Clairemont, Bay Ho, Bay Park, Linda Vista, Kearny Mesa, and Serra Mesa neighborhoods of San Diego, CA. The store is hosted on a subdomain (`store.exploreclairemont.com`) and is powered by **Zylvie**, a third-party cart/storefront platform — it is **not** a native WooCommerce or Shopify store.

The store functions primarily as a **B2B marketing and services marketplace**, offering local businesses and community members ways to promote themselves, access partnership materials, and claim exclusive local deals. The vast majority of products are free (digital downloads or coupons), with a small set of paid listings and ad placements.

## Pricing Structure Summary

| Type | Price Range | Notes |
|---|---|---|
| Free downloads / resources | FREE | PDFs, flyers, guides, coupons |
| Free listings | FREE | Basic directory & event listings |
| Paid event listings | $20 – $150 one-time | Per-event, tiered feature set |

The store is **primarily a freemium model** — most items are free to reduce friction for businesses and community members to engage with the EC ecosystem. Paid products are modest in cost and designed for small local businesses.

## Connection to ExploreClairemont.com (Main Site)

The store is tightly integrated with the main site:

- **"Back to EXPLORE CLAIREMONT"** link on every store page leads to `exploreclairemont.com`
- **Branding is consistent** — same logo, color palette (purple/green), and EC identity
- **Products directly drive traffic/listings on the main site** — directory listing and event listing purchases result in entries on `exploreclairemont.com/directory/` and the events calendar
- **Explorer Exclusives** tie in local partner businesses also featured in the main directory (e.g., Indya Restaurant, Little Backstube both appear as featured listings on the main site)
- **The Partner page** on the main site (`exploreclairemont.com/partner/`) mirrors the store's offerings (newsletter ads, directory upgrades, display ads, social collabs, content collaboration) as a form-based inquiry channel

## Business Model Summary

Explore Clairemont operates as a **hyperlocal media and directory platform** monetized through:

1. **Business directory listing upgrades** (Hometown Hero subscription)
2. **Event listing fees** (tiered one-time payments)
3. **Display advertising** on the main website
4. **Newsletter sponsorships** (sold via inquiry/partnership form)
5. **Explorer Exclusives** partnerships (businesses offer free deals to drive foot traffic)
6. **Content collaboration** (featured articles, social posts — sold via partnership form)

The store serves as the **self-serve purchasing layer**, while the main site's Partner page handles custom/larger partnership inquiries. The free resources (guides, flyers, media kit) serve as lead-generation tools to attract businesses and residents into the EC ecosystem.
