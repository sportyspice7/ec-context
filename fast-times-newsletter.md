# Fast Times — Newsletter Guide

## The Basics
- **Name:** Fast Times
- **Frequency:** Weekly
- **Platform:** Email newsletter
- **Publisher:** Tanja, via ExploreClairemont.com

## Signature Format
- **Always opens with:** "Hey Neighbor!"
- Warm, personal, like a note from a friend who knows the neighborhood

## Coverage Areas
The newsletter covers the broader Clairemont community, including:
- Clairemont (core)
- Linda Vista
- Bay Ho
- Bay Park
- Kearny Mesa
- Serra Mesa
- Birdland

## Typical Content Mix (in rough priority order)
- Local news & stories
- Events & things to do
- Business spotlights and new openings
- Community announcements & deals (Explorer Exclusives)

## Voice & Tone
See `tone-and-voice.md` for full details, but in summary:
- Warm and neighborly
- Community-first
- Written as if from a trusted neighbor, not a publication
- Celebratory of local life
- Never corporate, never cold

## Newsletter Goals
- Keep residents informed about what's happening locally
- Drive engagement with local businesses
- Strengthen the sense of community and belonging
- Be the weekly touchpoint neighbors look forward to

## Notes for Claude
- When writing Fast Times content, always start with "Hey Neighbor!"
- Keep the tone conversational and warm — not journalistic or formal
- Hyperlocal specificity matters — mention real neighborhoods, real business names, real community touchpoints
- Avoid generic "San Diego" framing — this is specifically about Clairemont and surrounding neighborhoods
