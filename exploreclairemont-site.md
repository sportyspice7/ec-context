# ExploreClairemont.com — Site Overview

## What It Is
ExploreClairemont.com is a **hyperlocal community website** for the Clairemont area of San Diego, California. It serves as a digital hub connecting neighbors, supporting local businesses, and celebrating the community.

## Core Identity
- **Type:** Hyperlocal community media site
- **Platform:** WordPress
- **Focus area:** Clairemont, San Diego
- **Zip codes covered:** 92110, 92111, 92117, 92123
- **Neighborhoods covered:** Clairemont, Linda Vista, Bay Ho, Bay Park, Kearny Mesa, Serra Mesa, Birdland

## Site Sections & Features

### Business & Events Directories
Local business listings and community event calendar for the Clairemont area.

### Explorer Exclusives
A deals/offers section — exclusive discounts and promotions from local businesses for community members.

### ClaireShare
A social community feature — a space for neighbors to share, connect, and engage with each other.

### Fast Times Newsletter
Weekly email newsletter. See `fast-times-newsletter.md` for full details.

### News & Stories
Original articles and local news written by Tanja — community journalism and storytelling.

### Neighborhood Guides
In-depth guides to the neighborhoods within the Clairemont area.

### Local Scroll
A community photo section — visual content submitted by or featuring community members.

### Partner Programs
Business partnership and sponsorship opportunities for local businesses to be featured or promoted.

## Additional Properties
- **Store:** store.exploreclairemont.com
- **Social media:** Instagram, Facebook, TikTok — all @exploreclairemont

## Mission
To be the go-to hyperlocal hub for Clairemont — connecting neighbors, championing local businesses, and making community life richer. The site also generates revenue through advertising and partnerships with local businesses.

## Audience
- **Primary:** Both long-time Clairemont residents (rooted, community-invested) and newcomers (getting to know the neighborhood)
- **Secondary:** Local business owners and anyone connected to the Clairemont area

## Technical Notes
- Built on WordPress
- Tanja has developed custom plugins for the site
- Uses GitHub for version control of plugins and site code
