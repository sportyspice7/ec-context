## Neighborhood Data

### Bay Ho
- Population: 17,116 | Avg Age: 42
- Median Household Income: $127,138 | Median Home Price: $1,219,089
- Known for: San Diego Harley Davidson, Costco, Price Club origins (1976)
- Fun facts: Price Club (world's first membership warehouse) founded here; motocross legend Marty Smith was a resident

### Bay Park
- Population: 17,445 | Avg Age: 41
- Median Household Income: $114,483 | Median Home Price: $1,451,353
- Known for: Morena Boulevard, Mission Bay views, Bay Park Fish Company, Harland Brewing
- Fun facts: Jim Morrison went to Bay Park Elementary; Morena Blvd was original Route 101

### Birdland
- Population: 4,301 | Avg Age: 36
- Median Household Income: $104,095 | Median Home Price: $954,557
- Known for: Streets named after birds, proximity to Sharp and Rady Children's Hospitals
- Post-WWII development, 1950s-60s homes

### Clairemont
- Population: 95,522 | Avg Age: 40
- Median Household Income: $105,286 | Median Home Price: $1,166,481
- Known for: Nation's first master-planned single-family community, Tecolote Canyon
- Fun facts: Fast Times at Ridgemont High based on Clairemont High; Kris Jenner, Kendra Wilkinson, Mark Hamill are alumni
- Annual events: Family Day (September); 75th birthday celebration at Clairemont Town Square (2025)

### Kearny Mesa
- Population: 5,042 | Avg Age: 35
- Median Household Income: $103,189 | Median Home Price: $869,385
- Known for: Convoy District (top Asian food destination), Jack in the Box HQ
- History: Started as Camp Kearny military base (1917), aerospace hub, Pan-Asian influence since 1990s

### Linda Vista
- Population: 23,353 | Avg Age: 36
- Median Household Income: $94,231 | Median Home Price: $876,085
- Known for: Vietnamese restaurants, USD, Home Brew Mart, cultural diversity
- Fun facts: First San Diego shopping mall (1942); infamous 1995 tank rampage started here
- Annual event: Multicultural Fair & Parade (April, Linda Vista Road)

### Serra Mesa
- Population: 35,567 | Avg Age: 39
- Median Household Income: $106,261 | Median Home Price: $975,143
- Known for: Sharp Healthcare campus, Ruffin Canyon trails, family neighborhood
- Fun facts: Named after Junípero Serra; Rick Harrison (Pawn Stars) is from here; Haveteur Way named from a marital dispute
- Annual event: Serra Mesa Big Block Party (July, Greyling Drive)
